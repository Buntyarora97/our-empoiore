# Our Empire Landing Page

This folder contains the standalone landing page for the Our Empire player app.

## Setup

1. Copy your APK file (rename it `OurEmpire.apk`) into this folder alongside `index.html`
2. Update the contact links in `index.html`:
   - WhatsApp: `https://wa.me/91XXXXXXXXXX` — replace with your number
   - Telegram: `https://t.me/yourchannelname` — replace with your channel
3. Upload entire folder contents to Hostinger (public_html directory)

## Deploy to Hostinger

1. Log into Hostinger hPanel
2. Go to **File Manager** → `public_html`
3. Upload `index.html` and `OurEmpire.apk`
4. Your page is live at your domain!

## To share

Share your Hostinger domain link: `https://yourdomain.com`  
Users click "Download APK" and install directly.
